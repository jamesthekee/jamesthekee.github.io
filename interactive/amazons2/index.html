Game of the Amazons

Rules & Controls

How to Play

Game of the Amazons is a two-player strategy game played on a grid. Each player controls 2-4 Amazons depending on the board size .

  1. Players take alternating turns, each turn involves moving an amazon and shooting an arrow.
  2. Move an Amazon like a chess queen (any distance in 8 directions).
  3. Shoot an arrow from your Amazon's new position - also like a queen move.
  4. Arrows block squares permanently for the rest of the game. You cannot move or shoot through an arrow.
  5. The player who cannot move loses.

Controls

  • Click an Amazon to select it, then click a square to move.
  • After moving, click a square to shoot your arrow.
  • Right-click to undo the current partial move.
  • Undo / Redo buttons step through full moves.

Settings

  • Show moves — highlights valid squares for the selected Amazon.
  • Show last move — draws an arrow showing the previous move.
